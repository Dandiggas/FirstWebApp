Biography

Hailing from South-East London, Dan Diggas has in recent years made a name for himself. His production credits span across a wide range of genres; including the current number 1 album by Central Cee, Mahalia, Sinead Harnett, Lucky Daye, SG Lewis and Tiana Major9. With a sound rooted in Gospel, Electronica, Hip Hop and R&B, his production serves a unique catalogue of chord progressions, and sonics. Dan began his career as a keyboard player, drawing inspiration from Joe Sample and Herbie Hancock. Dan Diggas has gained over 100 million streams across his releases. 2022 is a promising year for Dan Diggas, as he continues to expand his catalogue.
